ABOUT AIRDRONE-RIGPPD 2025

AIRDRONE-RIGPPD is a club dedicated to exploring the fields of drones and robotics.
Our activities cover various areas including programming, technical skills, and other disciplines
related to robotics—both directly and indirectly.
The main goal of the club is to explore, learn, and share knowledge in the world of modern technology.

Established in 2025,
AIRDRONE-RIGPPD recently recruited around 13 passionate students and 2 dedicated lecturers.
Together, we aim to grow as a community that builds, innovates, and pushes
the boundaries of what’s possible with robotics and drone technology.
